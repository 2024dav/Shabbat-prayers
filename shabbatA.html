Blessing the Family

For the Son

Adonai will make you like Ephraim and Manasseh
Our Father in heaven will instill in your heart His Love and Reverence

For the Daughter

Adonai will make you like Sarah, Rebecca, Rachel and Leah and even Ruth
who built the house of Israel.
Our Father in Heaven will instill in your heart His Love and Reverence
Adonai will bless you and safe guard you and make peace for you.

Aaronic Blessing Numbers 6:24

The Lord will Bless you and He will keep you.
The Lord will make His Face to Shine upon you and He will be gracious to you.
The Lord lift His Countenance to you and He will establish you in shalom.
